NovaTech Inc. IT Usage Policy

1. Company Devices
Laptops, phones, and tablets provided by NovaTech are for business use. Minimal personal use is permitted provided it does not interfere with work or violate policies.

2. Software Installation
Users are prohibited from installing unauthorized software on company devices. Request IT support for any software needs.

3. Password Policy
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, alphanumeric with special characters, and changed every 90 days. Sharing passwords is strictly forbidden.

4. Email Usage
Corporate email should not be used for harassment, chain letters, or signing up for personal services not related to work.

5. Monitoring
NovaTech reserves the right to monitor internet usage, email, and files stored on company devices to ensure compliance with policies.
