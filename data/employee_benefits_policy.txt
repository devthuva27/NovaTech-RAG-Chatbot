NovaTech Inc. Employee Benefits Policy

1. Health Insurance
NovaTech provides comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance for employees and their dependents. The company covers 80% of the premium.

2. Retirement Plan (401k)
Employees are eligible to join the 401k plan after 3 months of employment. NovaTech matches employee contributions up to 5% of their salary.

3. Gym Membership
All employees receive a subsidized gym membership at local partner fitness centers (Gold's Gym, Planet Fitness). Reimbursement is up to $50/month.

4. Commuter Benefits
Pre-tax deductions are available for public transit passes. Parking is provided free of charge at the main office.

5. Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
Confidential counseling services are available 24/7 for employees facing personal or work-related challenges.
