NovaTech Inc. Leave Policy

1. Annual Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Leave accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month. Unused leave up to 10 days can be carried forward to the next calendar year.

2. Sick Leave
Employees are granted 10 days of paid sick leave annually. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days.

3. Parental Leave
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks of paid leave.
- Paternity Leave: 4 weeks of paid leave.
- Adoption Leave: 12 weeks of paid leave.

4. Public Holidays
NovaTech observes all national public holidays. Employees required to work on a public holiday will receive 200% of their daily wage or a compensatory day off.

5. Unpaid Leave
Requests for unpaid leave must be approved by the Department Head and HR. It is generally granted only in exceptional circumstances.
