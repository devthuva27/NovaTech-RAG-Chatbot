NovaTech Inc. Working Hours Policy

1. Standard Hours
Standard business hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, with a one-hour lunch break.

2. Flexible Hours
Employees may request flexible start and end times (e.g., 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM or 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM). Core hours where all employees must be available are 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM.

3. Overtime
- Non-Exempt Employees: Eligible for overtime pay at 1.5x hourly rate for hours worked beyond 40 per week.
- Exempt Employees: Not eligible for overtime pay but may be granted time off in lieu (TOIL) for excessive hours during crunch periods.

4. Breaks
Employees are entitled to two 15-minute paid breaks per day in addition to the unpaid lunch hour.
