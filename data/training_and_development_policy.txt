NovaTech Inc. Training and Development Policy

1. Budget
Each department has a training budget. Employees can request up to $2,000 annually for courses, certifications, or conferences relevant to their role.

2. Approval
Requests must be submitted to the manager at least 2 weeks in advance. Justification of ROI (Return on Investment) for the team is required.

3. Study Leave
Employees pursuing job-related degrees or certifications may be granted up to 3 days of paid study leave per year before exams.

4. Internal Training
Mandatory internal training (e.g., Compliance, Cybersecurity) must be completed within 30 days of assignment.

5. Mentorship Program
Senior employees are encouraged to mentor juniors. Mentors receive an annual bonus of $1,000 for verified mentorship participation.
