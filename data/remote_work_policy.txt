NovaTech Inc. Remote Work Policy

1. Eligibility
Remote work is available to employees whose roles can be effectively performed off-site. Assessment is done by the immediate manager.

2. Hybrid Model
NovaTech operates on a hybrid model. Employees are expected to be in the office at least 2 days a week (specifically Tuesdays and Thursdays for team collaboration).

3. Working Hours
Remote employees must adhere to standard working hours (9:00 AM - 6:00 PM) and be available for communication via Slack and Email during this time.

4. Home Office Setup
Employees are responsible for creating a secure and distraction-free work environment. NovaTech provides a one-time stipend of $500 for home office equipment.

5. Data Security
Employees must use company-issued devices for work. Use of unsecured public Wi-Fi is strictly prohibited. VPN connection is mandatory when accessing company systems.
