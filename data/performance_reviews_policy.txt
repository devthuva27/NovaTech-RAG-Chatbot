NovaTech Inc. Performance Reviews Policy

1. Cycle
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year:
- Mid-Year Review: July
- Annual Review: January

2. Ratings Scale
1 - Does Not Meet Expectations
2 - Partially Meets Expectations
3 - Meets Expectations
4 - Exceeds Expectations
5 - Outstanding

3. Process
- Self-Assessment: Employee submits their own review.
- Peer Review: Feedback from close collaborators (3 peers).
- Manager Assessment: Manager compiles all data and provides final rating.
- 1:1 Meeting: Discussion of results and goal setting for next period.

4. Promotions and Raises
Salary adjustments and promotions are decided based on the Annual Review outcome.
