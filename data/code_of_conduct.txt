NovaTech Inc. Code of Conduct

1. Professionalism
Employees must treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect. Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind (verbal, physical, distinct) will not be tolerated.

2. Integrity
We expect honesty in all business dealings. Fraud, theft, or deliberate falsification of records serves as grounds for immediate termination.

3. Conflict of Interest
Employees must avoid situations where personal interests conflict with company duties. Any potential conflict must be disclosed to HR immediately.

4. Dress Code
Business casual is the standard dress code. Smart casual is acceptable on Fridays.

5. Substance Abuse
Possession or use of illegal drugs or alcohol on company premises is strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action.
