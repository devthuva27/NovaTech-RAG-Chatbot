NovaTech Inc. Expense Reimbursement Policy

1. Reimbursable Expenses
- Travel: Flights (economy class), trains, and taxis for business trips.
- Accommodation: Hotel stays capped at $150/night.
- Meals: Daily allowance of $50 during business travel.
- Client Entertainment: Must be pre-approved by a manager.

2. Non-Reimbursable Expenses
- Personal items or services (e.g., movies, gym duties).
- Traffic fines or parking tickets.
- Alcohol (unless part of an approved client dinner).

3. Submission Process
Expenses must be submitted via the Zoho Expense portal within 30 days of incurring the cost. Original receipts are required for all expenses over $10.

4. Approval
Managers must approve expenses within 5 working days. Reimbursement is processed in the next payroll cycle.
