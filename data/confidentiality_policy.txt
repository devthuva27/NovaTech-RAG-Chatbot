NovaTech Inc. Confidentiality Policy

1. Definition
Confidential information includes client data, trade secrets, software code, financial records, and employee details.

2. Obligations
- Employees must not disclose confidential information to unauthorized persons inside or outside the company.
- Confidential information should only be accessed for legitimate business purposes.

3. Post-Employment
The obligation to maintain confidentiality continues indefinitely after employment ends. Return of all company data and documents is mandatory upon departure.

4. Breach
Any breach of this policy may result in legal action and/or termination of employment.

5. NDAs
Employees may be required to sign Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) for specific projects.
